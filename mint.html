Mint

The owner of the contract is able create new ERC1155 and ERC721 artifacts here the smart contract is keeping track of our assets ID and as a result has been ommited.

When minting a new Enjimon our creature will need a Name, Health level (HP) Defense level (DEF), attack level (ATK), Endurance level (Endur), starting level (LVL), and Enjimon Type. Once filled hitting the mint button will prompt the web3 to ask for verifaction via signature. The correspond gas fee tx must then be paid as required by the Ethereum Network.



Enjimon Tokens are capped and hardcoded at 10,000,000 Total Token Supply. only new living digital assets, and artifacts can be minted. Only contract owner can utilize mint function
