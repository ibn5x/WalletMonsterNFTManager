Transfer
Only the holder of the digital asset can freely transfer thier asset. To do so, insert the token id (in most cases this will be prefiled) of the asset your attempting to trade, the amount, and recipient address then hit the transfer button. You will be prompted to verify this transaction by signing with your Web3 based wallet and paying any gas tx fee.

Dead Enjimon cannot be transfered out of wallet
