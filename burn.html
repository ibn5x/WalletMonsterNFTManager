Burn

To burn your asset(s) insert the token id (in most cases this will be prefiled) of the asset your attempting to burn, (you will only be able to destroy tokens you own) followed by the amount. We have taken care to prefill the owner address input. Clicking the burn button will trigger your web3 wallet to have you verify this action by signing. You will then have to pay the gas fee TX as required by the Ethereum Network.



Only Holder of asset can burn their token. Only contract owner can burn token supply
